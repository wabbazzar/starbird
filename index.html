Your money funds their playbook.

Private equity firms buy beloved brands, load them with debt, cut costs (read: workers), then sell for a profit — usually within 3–5 years. The brand, the workers, and often the customers are left with the wreckage.

This app tracks PE-owned consumer brands, explains why it matters, and suggests independent alternatives. Knowledge is leverage.

The numbers

In 2024, PE-related bankruptcies directly caused 65,850+ layoffs. PE-backed companies were behind 56% of all large bankruptcies (those with $500M+ in liabilities), despite representing only ~6.5% of the US economy. Studies show PE-acquired companies are 10× more likely to go bankrupt than comparable non-PE firms.
